Review | Extraction II

Dominic Morgado August 10, 2023

Extraction II continues along from the first one with all the extra packed shooting and fight scenes. It picks up from the results of the ending of the first movie with Tyler (Chris Hemsworth) recovering/retiring. He is then approached by Alcott with a mission he cannot turn away due to guilt and things that has happened in his past. There are two recurring characters from the first part that join the mission along with Tyler. Not only do you get to witness the quick backstory of the “good guys” you get the same for the bad guys. From the start of their childhood and the events of their upbringing and you can see why they are who the are and what they do. It's a product of their environment in a sense which people can relate to.

All the fighting/shooting scenes keep you very entertained and, on your heels, wondering what's going to happen next. Golshifteh Farahani, Adam Bessa, and Tornike Gogrichiani are a stellar cast alongside Chris Hemsworth and they all compliment one another perfectly. You sometimes can tell what will happen next in certain scenes but regardless of that it is a very solid action movie and a good follow up sequel. I am excited to see what is in store for the future of this franchise. If you enjoyed the Extracion you will enjoy this one just as much.

Trivia & Fun Facts about Extraction II

  • The one-take action sequence during the first act of the film is 21 minutes long. It took 4-5 months to rehearse and 29 days to shoot
  • Part of the prison scene was filmed in the same location where they shot the opening sequence of Mission: Impossible - Ghost Protocol
  • Golshifteh Farahani said in an interview that Extraction II had the least visual effects and they did almost all the scenes without stuntmen. She said Team Ka actually parked a rat helicopter on the train.
  • Tyler is seen watching a game of AFL(Austrialian Rules Football) early in the film. The team playing are the Brisbane Lions and Western Bulldogs. Chris Hemsworth is a fan of the Bulldogs and attended the grand final in 2016 that won them their second AFL/VFL premiership having not won one since 1954 which was their first.
